Guanghui Wang is currently a Visiting Research Student at the University of Victoria (UVic) , Victoria, BC, Canada, under supervision of Prof. Jianping Pan . He is also a PhD student at Nanjing University of Posts and Telecommunications (NUPT) , Nanjing, P. R. China, with Prof. Subin Shen . His research interests include algorithm design for privacy-preserving localization in pervasice computing, epsilon-delta data privacy analysis in localization, and distance distribution based performance analysis for localization. He is a student member of IEEE.
